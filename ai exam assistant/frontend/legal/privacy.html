Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

1. Data We Process

When you upload a PDF document, our system extracts the text content. This text is sent to our AI processing partner (OpenRouter/OpenAI) to generate summaries, answers, and quizzes.

2. Data Storage

Your uploaded documents are not stored permanently on our servers. Extracted text is held in temporary session memory and is cleared when you click "New Chat" or close your session. We do not build profiles based on your data.

3. Third-Party Services

We use OpenRouter and GPT-3.5 Turbo to provide AI responses. By using this tool, your document's text content is shared with these providers for processing purposes only. Please do not upload documents containing sensitive personal information, passwords, or confidential data.

4. Local Storage

We use your browser's LocalStorage to save your chat history locally so you don't lose progress if you refresh the page. This data stays on your device.

5. Your Consent

By using Exmora, you consent to this Privacy Policy and the processing of your data as described above.
